Prionailurus

^

Prionailurus is a genus of small, spotted wild cats native to Asia. Forests are their preferred habitat; they feed on small mammals, reptiles and birds, and occasionally aquatic wildlife. There are five species now recognised.

Prionailurus bengalensis
Prionailurus javanensis
Prionailurus planiceps
Prionailurus viverrinus
Prionailurus rubiginosus
