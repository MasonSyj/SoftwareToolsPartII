Marbled cat

^

The marbled cat (Pardofelis marmorata) is a small wild cat native from the eastern Himalayas to Southeast Asia, where it inhabits forests up to an elevation of 2,500 m (8,200 ft). As it is present in a large range, it has been listed as Near Threatened on the IUCN Red List since 2015. At present, two subspecies are recognized as valid.

Pardofelis marmorata marmorata
Pardofelis marmorata longicaudata
