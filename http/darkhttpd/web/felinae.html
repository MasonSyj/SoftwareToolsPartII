Felinae

^

The Felinae are a subfamily of the family Felidae. This subfamily comprises the small cats having a bony hyoid, because of which they are able to purr but not roar. Today, the following living genera and species are recognised as belonging to the Felinae:

Acinonyx
Caracel
Catopuma
Felis
Herpailurus
Leopardus
Leptailurus
Lynx
Otocolobus
Pardofelis
Prionailurus
Puma
